How We Found It
We make it a priority to find the most sustainable and environmentally friendly factories for our platform, they’re not always easy to find. In the case of our wool factory, a chance encounter in Hong Kong set us on a hunt for this factory in mainland China. Wool is a premium product and we knew Factory Window has to offer the same factories used by top brands. Luckily, we met the lead designer of this factory at an exhibition, who took the time to learn more about us and loved the concept!
Capabilities
They are capable of producing 80,000 units per month and currently employ over 500 employees in a beautiful factory. Minimum quantity orders are as low as 50 pieces giving a wide range of designers and brands an opportunity to work with such an established market player.
The Materials
This factory shepherds their own flock of sheep, which they raise, shear, and clean before spinning the wool themselves. The vertical integration allows the factory to control costs and maximize returns—ensuring the best, most efficient manufacturing process possible, which is of course sustainable.
About the Factory
In its 50 year history, this factory has been owned by two families. It was started by a local weaver, who opened a mill and sold it to a family friend, who has continued to operate the business. The family is dedicated to maintaining their quality and craftsmanship and treating employees well. Established in 1967, this factory employs 800 people in the city of Dongguan. The grounds of the factory are simple, yet elegant. On our last visit we toured the local cafeteria and discussed what many workers do for fun. On the weekends, they often run into town for movies or play Mahjong in the courtyard.
